UK Dog Marketplace Analysis

100% of under-8-week puppies on P4H & Gumtree
have future ready-to-leave dates set

UK Law: 8-Week Minimum
8
Weeks minimum age
Under the Animal Welfare Regulations 2018 (Lucy's Law), puppies cannot leave their mother before 8 weeks. Early separation causes behavioural issues, anxiety, and health problems. Platforms should enforce future ready-to-leave dates for young puppies.
Ready-to-Leave Enforcement
Platform <8wk Protected %
Pets4Homes 2,433 Enforced 100%
Gumtree 516 Enforced 100%

How Platforms Protect Puppies
Pets4Homes & Gumtree
Mandatory ready-to-leave field. 100% of under-8-week puppies have future dates set. Buyers cannot collect early.
FreeAds Gap
559 puppies under 8 weeks — 77% have no ready date. Of those with dates, many say "Now". Field is optional with no enforcement.
